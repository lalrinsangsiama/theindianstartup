üìö Funding Glossary for Beginners

Angel Investor
Individual who invests personal money in early-stage startups, typically ‚Çπ25L-‚Çπ2Cr range.
Venture Capital (VC)
Professional investment firm that invests larger amounts (‚Çπ2Cr+) in exchange for equity.
Valuation
The estimated worth of your company, determines how much equity you give for investment.
Equity
Ownership percentage in your company that you give to investors in exchange for money.
Term Sheet
Document outlining key terms of investment - amount, valuation, rights, etc.
Due Diligence
Detailed investigation by investors of your business, finances, and legal structure.
Runway
How long your current money will last based on monthly burn rate.
Series A, B, C
Funding rounds as company grows - A (early growth), B (scaling), C (expansion).
Convertible Note
Loan that converts to equity in future funding round, common for early-stage funding.
SAFE Agreement
Simple Agreement for Future Equity - investor gets equity when certain events occur.
