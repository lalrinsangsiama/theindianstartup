Title Slide

10 seconds to hook

💡 Pro Tips for Title Slide

  • Your tagline should explain what you do in 6-8 words
  • Make it memorable and easy to understand
  • Avoid jargon - your grandmother should understand it
  • Include contact info for follow-up

Your registered company name

One-line description that anyone can understand

✨ Great Tagline Examples

Airbnb: "Book rooms with locals, rather than hotels"
Uber: "Push a button, get a ride"
Slack: "Where work happens"

The Problem

Make them feel the pain

💡 Pro Tips for Problem Slide

  • Use data to quantify the problem size
  • Make it relatable with real examples
  • Show why existing solutions fail
  • Create urgency - why solve this NOW?

2-3 sentences that clearly define the problem

Specific target audience size and description

List 2-3 existing solutions and their limitations

Economic or social cost of the problem

Your Solution

The "Aha!" moment

💡 Pro Tips for Solution Slide

  • Show, don't just tell - use visuals/screenshots
  • Explain your unique approach
  • Focus on benefits, not features
  • Keep it simple - avoid technical jargon

2-3 sentences explaining your solution clearly

List your 3 most important features

Your unfair advantage or unique approach

Market Opportunity

Show the billion-dollar potential

💡 Pro Tips for Market Slide

  • Use TAM → SAM → SOM framework
  • Show market growth rate (CAGR)
  • Bottom-up calculation is more credible
  • Cite credible sources (reports, studies)

Total market size

Your reachable market

Realistic 5-year target

Annual growth rate and future projections

Business Model

How you make money

💡 Pro Tips for Business Model Slide

  • Show multiple revenue streams if applicable
  • Include pricing strategy and rationale
  • Demonstrate unit economics
  • Show path to profitability

How you charge and what percentage each contributes

When and how you'll become profitable

Traction & Validation

Proof it's working

💡 Pro Tips for Traction Slide

  • Use graphs to show growth trajectory
  • Include both vanity and meaningful metrics
  • Show month-over-month growth rates
  • Include customer testimonials or logos

List 3-5 impressive achievements

One powerful customer story

Competition & Differentiation

Why you'll win

💡 Pro Tips for Competition Slide

  • Never say "we have no competition"
  • Use a comparison matrix or positioning map
  • Show respect for competitors
  • Highlight your unique advantages

List competitors and their focus

Why customers choose you over others

What prevents others from copying you

The Team

Why you're the ones to do this

💡 Pro Tips for Team Slide

  • Highlight relevant experience and achievements
  • Show domain expertise and past successes
  • Include key advisors if impressive
  • Show complementary skills across founders

Include notable advisors and team size

Financial Projections

The numbers that matter

💡 Pro Tips for Financials Slide

  • Show 3-year projections (5 years is too speculative)
  • Include key assumptions below projections
  • Be aggressive but realistic
  • Show path to profitability clearly

The Ask & Use of Funds

What you need to succeed

💡 Pro Tips for Ask Slide

  • Be specific about the amount and terms
  • Show how funds accelerate growth
  • Include runway this gives you (18-24 months ideal)
  • Show expected milestones with this funding

Show percentage and specific use for each category

What you'll achieve with this investment

Include amount, investors, and year
