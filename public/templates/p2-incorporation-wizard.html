💡 Name Availability Check

Your company name will be checked for availability on MCA portal. Keep 2-3 backup names ready. Names should be unique and not identical to existing companies.

⚠️ Capital Structure Tips

Keep authorized capital reasonable initially. You can increase it later. Higher authorized capital means higher stamp duty. For startups, ₹10 lakhs authorized capital is usually sufficient.

Private Limited companies need minimum 2 directors

📋 Required Documents per Director/Shareholder
