💡 Data Room Best Practices
  • Organize systematically: Use clear folder structure and consistent naming
  • Update regularly: Keep documents current and remove outdated versions
  • Control access: Set appropriate permissions for different users
  • Track activity: Monitor who accesses what documents and when
  • Prepare summaries: Include executive summaries for complex documents
  • Redact sensitive info: Remove confidential information from early-stage documents
